The Story of Beija Flor

Beija Flor coffee is sourced from the lush mountains of Minas Gerais, Brazil. This renowned coffee-growing region is known for its ideal climate and rich volcanic soil.

The unique terroir of Minas Gerais, combined with our careful cultivation methods, results in Beija Flor's distinctive flavor profile - a harmonious blend of caramel sweetness, subtle spice notes, and a smooth, wine-like finish.
