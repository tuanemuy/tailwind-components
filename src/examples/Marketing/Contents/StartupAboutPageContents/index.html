The story behind Preline

Prelin is a web company that builds websites. Businesses of every size—from new startups to public companies—use our theme to build and manage their businesses online.

Tools should adapt to the user, not the other way around.

Preline is a powerful and versatile open-source library of prebuilt UI components based on the Tailwind CSS framework. With over 750 accessible and customizable components, templates, and examples, Preline empowers developers to create visually stunning and fully responsive, mobile-first websites with ease.

In addition to its robust component library, Preline offers a comprehensive Figma design system for seamless design-to-development workflows. Whether you're a developer or a designer, Preline provides the tools to streamline your projects and elevate your web creations.

Our offerings go beyond components—we specialize in custom web design and development services, including UI/UX design, starter templates, detailed documentation, and personalized support. Backed by a community-driven approach, Preline is here to help you build with confidence and creativity.
